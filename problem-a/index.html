Kratos: The Greek God

Kratos, also known as Cratus or Cratos, is the greek personification of strenth. Kratos is the god of war. He is the son of Pallas, and Styx.

  1. Kratos has a lot of resilience. Although a lot of Gods want to kill him, He always finds a way to rise up, and be better
  2. Kratos cares a lot about his wife, and even though she gave him the monumental task to spread her ashes from the highest point in the world, Kratos fulfills it with rigor
  3. Kratos loves his son and will do anything for him because family always comes first. Loki is his son.
